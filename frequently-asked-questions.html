Have a question? We can help.
Q: What is an escape room?
A: An escape room is a unique, group experience where participants are enclosed in a room where they have a limited amount of time to work together to solve puzzles in order to escape or achieve an objective.
Q: How long will the game last?
A: The game typically lasts 60 minutes.
Q: Are there any physical requirements to play?
A: Our experiences are mental challenges and require very little physical effort.
Q: Are there any age restrictions to play?
A: We do not have any age restrictions to play; however, we recommend that players be at least 14 years of age because some of our puzzles are quite challenging.
Q: How much does it cost?
A: Tickets cost $25 per person.
Q: Can I buy a gift certificate?
A: Of course! Gift vouchers are available through our reservations page.
Q: Where are you located?
A: We are located on the bottom level of the Ivy Square Shopping Center next to Foods of All Nations.
Q: Where can I park?
A: Convenient parking is located on the lower level of our building.
Q: What if I don't have enough people in my group?
A: If you do not have a full group, we might pair you with new friends to help.
